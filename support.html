Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect with friends?
Launch PointerPals and share your connection URL with friends. They can join by opening the URL in their PointerPals app.
Why can't I see my friend's cursor?
Make sure both you and your friend are connected to the same session and that your friend is actively moving their cursor. Cursors fade out when inactive.
Is my data private?
Yes! PointerPals only shares cursor positions with connected friends. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.
How do I report a bug or request a feature?
Please email us at jbobrow+pointerpals@gmail.com with details about the bug or your feature request.
What macOS versions are supported?
PointerPals requires macOS 13.0 (Ventura) or later.
